Getting Here

Book your flight to Montego Bay’s Sangster International Airport (MBJ). Let us provide you with the contact information for a trusted Taxi. Book your Taxi in advance and have your driver waiting for you at the airport when you arrive! You’ll be on our private beach in 90 minutes. Driver fees to and from the airport are $80 for up to two passengers and $30 for each additional passenger.


Directions: Drive to Negril, go around the circle and follow West End road (aka One Love Drive) all the way around the cliffs. You will come to a split in the road right past the Out of Town Bakery. Stay to your right and follow the road. You will pass Moondance on the Cliffs, Hide Awhile, Banton’s bar, and Sunset Pub. Just a bit further down the road on the left-hand side you will see the white wall and the big painted sign for Tingalaya’s Retreat. You have arrived!
